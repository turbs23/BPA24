Meet the Fright Crew

Stage Fright isn't just a rock band; it's an explosive fusion of sound, emotion, and energy that leaves a lasting impression on everyone in the room. Known for their dynamic live performances and raw, authentic sound, Stage Fright connects with fans on a visceral level, drawing them into an intense, immersive experience. Every show is a powerful mix of driving rhythms, electrifying riffs, and a passion that's impossible to ignore.

Atharva is the creative force behind Stage Fright's unforgettable riffs and solos. His skill on the guitar adds layers to the band's sound, from haunting melodies to electrifying, high-energy riffs. With influences ranging from classic rock to modern alternative, Atharva brings versatility and emotion to each note, making every song a journey. His presence on stage is pure energy, pushing the band to new heights with every performance.

Abish is the voice and soul of Stage Fright. Known for his deep, resonant vocals and magnetic stage presence, he brings the band's lyrics to life with a raw intensity that captivates audiences. Abish's powerful voice and charismatic energy set the tone for every song, guiding fans through the highs and lows of the music. With an innate ability to connect with the crowd, he brings authenticity and depth to every performance.

Vedant is the backbone of Stage Fright's sound, driving the music forward with powerful, commanding beats. His drumming is the pulse that holds it all together, bringing intensity and precision that keeps audiences hooked. With a natural sense of rhythm and an unmistakable passion for his craft, Vedant's performance is a show in itself. He doesn't just keep time - he creates it, setting the stage for an unforgettable musical experience.
